Social Welfare and Development Committee

The Social Welfare and Development Committee of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering sustainable social change. Embracing the motto “Help One Inspire Many,” we have embarked on a journey to create a more empathetic and inclusive society. Our volunteers, primarily students aged 18-22, are driven by a profound sense of duty and a commitment to making a positive impact. Through various activities and initiatives, we strive to cultivate empathy, leadership skills, and selflessness among our members, all while commemorating their efforts for a better world.

  • OBJECTIVE:- Our objective is clear: to create an equal and just society where every individual, irrespective of background, has access to the resources and opportunities needed for a fulfilling life. We believe that even the smallest efforts, when combined, can lead to significant societal change. Upholding the values of empathy and gratitude, we provide students with a platform to give back to society. Our aim is to harness the enthusiasm, courage, and energy of youth towards meaningful causes, forming a community that advocates for inclusion and kindness. In this pursuit, our committee serves as a catalyst for positive change, working tirelessly to address social issues and uplift those in need. We strive to inspire others to join us in our mission, creating a ripple effect of compassion and goodwill.

  • VISION:- The vision of the Social Welfare and Development Committee is akin to a symphony of compassion, orchestrating harmonious endeavors for a better world. With passion as our conductor, we engage in a medley of activities, sowing seeds of hope in every corner of society. We envision a society where empathy is the cornerstone of interactions, where everyone has equal opportunities to thrive and succeed. Our vision extends beyond the boundaries of our campus, reaching out to the wider community and beyond. We aspire to be agents of change, empowering individuals to realize their full potential and creating a legacy of kindness and social responsibility.

  • MiSsion:- Our mission is deeply rooted in the values of service, integrity, and empathy. We are committed to enhancing the well-being and quality of life for all members of our society through a range of initiatives. From organizing skill-building workshops to awareness campaigns and various such activities, we are dedicated to making a tangible difference. Through our actions, we seek to inspire, educate, and empower individuals to become changemakers in their own right. We firmly believe that by investing in social welfare and development, we can build a brighter future for generations to come. Every act of kindness, every moment of empathy, is a step towards a more compassionate and inclusive society.
