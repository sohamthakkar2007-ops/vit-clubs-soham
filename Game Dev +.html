Game Dev +

Game dev + is established to expedite and introduce students to the development of both small-scale and large-scale 2d/3d game development along with a new concept of Gamification. Our goal is to familiarize students with various domains of game development and to develop a community of game development enthusiasts.

  • OBJECTIVE:- Create an environment for game development enthusiasts by introducing students to gamification and providing opportunities to explore the field of game development.

  • VISION and MISSION :- To empower aspiring game developers in India by fostering skill development, creativity, and collaboration in a supportive environment. To lead in nurturing game development talent through hands-on experiences, industry insights, and a collaborative space encouraging creativity & critical thinking.
