RangManch

Rangmanch is VIT, Pune’s only film-making club which aims to foster a passionate filmmaking community, fostering a deep interest and understanding of cinema and excellence in all aspects. It encourages creativity and empowers members to explore their visions. The club provides a platform for learning, collaboration and growth, allowing aspiring filmmakers to hone their skills, share their stories, and inspire others through the magic of cinema.

  • OBJECTIVE:- The ultimate goal of Rangmanch is to inculcate interest towards film-making and its expressions in the students, and to nurture a potent environment for budding artists.

  • VISION:- We have so many creative minds here at VIT. Let’s use that creativity in film-making!

  • MISSION:- Our mission is to incorporate the culture of filmmaking and to imbibe the knowledge of how films are made!
