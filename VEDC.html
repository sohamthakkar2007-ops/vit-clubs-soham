Entrepreneurship Development Cell

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell (V-EDC) of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, established in September 2013, is a student-run organization dedicated to nurturing the entrepreneurial spirit among college students. Our primary goal is to see at least 10% of graduating students transition into successful entrepreneurs. In the short term, we aim to provide platforms for students to interact with established entrepreneurs, impart entrepreneurial education through training and exercises, and facilitate engaging interactions with organizations to promote entrepreneurship.


  • OBJECTIVE:- For the long term, our objectives include motivating students to establish their own startups, developing business incubators, and creating corpus funds for seed ventures.

  • VISION:- Our vision is to produce successful entrepreneurs equipped with leadership qualities who use innovative and ethical business practices to make a global impact

  • MISSION:- our mission is to instill a passion for entrepreneurship among students.
