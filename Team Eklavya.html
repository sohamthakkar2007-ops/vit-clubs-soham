Eklavya

Team Eklavya is the official street play club of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology. It takes its very roots in expressing the best out of us through various social issues ultimately leading us to a better society.

  • OBJECTIVE:-
    • An awakening among the citizens against injustice and social evils like corruption, violence, etc.

    • Providing an entertaining reflection to the society and working towards a country of rationalism and awareness.

  • VISION:- Instill a sense of responsibility and raise awareness among the citizens regarding concerning social issues.

  • MISSION:- Express our ambition toward the growth of humanity through the art of street play.
